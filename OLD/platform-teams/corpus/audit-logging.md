# Audit Logging

Corpus manages centralized [audit logging](https://cloud.google.com/logging/docs/audit) resources. Google Cloud services write audit logs that record administrative activities and access within your Google Cloud resources.

Audit logs help you answer "who did what, where, and when?" within your Google Cloud organization. This is implemented as part of the CIS GCP Benchmark compliance controls in [pt-arche-google-project](https://github.com/osinfra-io/pt-arche-google-project) (CIS 2.1 and CIS 2.2).
