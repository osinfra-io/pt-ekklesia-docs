# Ethos — Philosophy

Ethos is the guiding philosophy that shapes platform principles. As a stream-aligned team, Ethos works directly on the flow of change to deliver value to end users, consuming the capabilities provided by the platform teams.

## Team Type

**Stream-Aligned Team** — Aligned to a single flow of work, delivering value independently while leveraging platform capabilities.

## Relationship to Platform Teams

Ethos consumes infrastructure and tooling from the platform teams:

- **Logos** — Organization structure, identity groups, and GitHub team membership
- **Corpus** — GCP projects, networking, and CI/CD service accounts
- **Pneuma** — Kubernetes clusters and service mesh
- **Arche** — Reusable OpenTofu modules
- **Techne** — Shared workflows and developer tooling
