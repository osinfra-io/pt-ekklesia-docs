# Mysterion

The keeper of hidden complexity — owning and evolving subsystems too intricate for any stream-aligned team to hold. Mysterion absorbs the specialist knowledge required to build and maintain the platform's most complex internals, revealing their workings to other teams through clear interfaces and deep collaboration.
