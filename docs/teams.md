# Teams

The platform is organized into teams, each with a distinct role. Teams are classified by type based on their relationship to the flow of change.

- **[Platform Teams](./platform-teams/index.md)** — Provide the foundational infrastructure, tooling, and services that stream-aligned teams depend on
- **[Stream-Aligned Teams](./stream-aligned-teams/index.md)** — Work directly on the flow of change to deliver value to end users, consuming platform capabilities
